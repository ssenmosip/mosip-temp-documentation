1.3 Targeted audience and reading suggestions

This Deployment Guide is intended to be used by people who will be responsible for planning, performing, or maintaining the deployment, such as the Systems Administrator, Chief Information Officer (CIO), Analysts, or Developers. It is intended that software support personnel can read this document and coordinate their efforts in the deployment of the application.
